Parmigiana Siciliana

Author by Alessia Cillari

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instruction

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Sprinkle salt on eggplant slices and let them sit for 30 minutes to release excess moisture. Pat them dry.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan, and fry eggplant slices until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  4. In a baking dish, layer eggplant slices, tomato sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat until ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
